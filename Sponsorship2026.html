About ISPH

Health matters to everyone ‚Äî yet because of differences in genetics, microbiome, culture, and lifestyle, no single set of health principles works for all. Nutrition, diet, and individual constitution profoundly influence wellness and treatment outcomes.

Precision Health integrates medicine, food science, nutrition, biotechnology, and artificial intelligence to deliver tailored health solutions for individuals and communities. This approach also helps the agriculture, food, and health industries allocate resources more effectively.

The International Society for Precision Health (ISPH) leverages its global network and big-data platforms to drive cross-disciplinary collaboration and promote health worldwide. ISPH 2026 will hold its annual conference from October 21 to 23 at National Ilan University, Taiwan. We welcome you to join us.
