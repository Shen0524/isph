Transport guide for international attendees of the ISPH 2026 Annual Conference.

Taipei → Jiaoxi ~60 min by train  ·  ~90 min by bus

📍 Your Two Destinations

🏨 Conference Hotel: Kilin Hotel Jiaoxi (麒麟山莊礁溪館), Jiaoxi Township — a hot-spring resort town. Alight at Jiaoxi Train Station (礁溪站) or Jiaoxi Transfer Station (礁溪轉運站).
🎓 Conference Venue: National Ilan University (國立宜蘭大學), Yilan City. Alight at Yilan Train Station (宜蘭站), ~15 min taxi or local bus to campus.
🚗 Jiaoxi and Yilan City are ~15 min apart by taxi (NT$200–250) or local bus 191.

Practical Tips
Before You Travel
🏨
Going to the Hotel (Jiaoxi)
Alight at Jiaoxi Train Station (礁溪站) if taking the train, or Jiaoxi Transfer Station (礁溪轉運站) if taking bus 1915, 1916, or 1572. Kilin Hotel Jiaoxi is a short ~5 min taxi ride (NT$80–120) from either stop.
🎓
Going to the Venue (Yilan)
Alight at Yilan Train Station (宜蘭站) or Yilan Transfer Station. National Ilan University is ~15 min by taxi (NT$150–200) or take local bus 79 / 241. From the hotel in Jiaoxi, taxi to NIU takes ~20 min (NT$200–260).
🎟️
Book Train Seats Early
Express seats (Puyuma, Taroko, Tze-Chiang 3000) sell out quickly around Oct 21–23. Book directly via TRA up to 28 days ahead, or via Klook up to 180 days ahead. Select Jiaoxi (礁溪) as your destination for the hotel.
💳
EasyCard for Buses
Kamalan Bus accepts the Taipei EasyCard (悠遊卡) — tap on board, no ticket needed. Get one at any Taipei MRT station (NT$100 refundable deposit, load any amount).
✈️
Direct from Airport (TPE)
Bus 1661 (Ubus) runs directly from Taoyuan Airport (TPE) to Yilan and Luodong — ~2.5 to 3 hrs, NT$300. Get off at Jiaoxi Transfer Station for the hotel. No need to go into Taipei first.
📱
TRA Mobile App
Download "Taiwan Railway e-booking" (iOS / Android). Has English support. International visitors can use their passport number as the booking ID instead of a Taiwan ID card.
