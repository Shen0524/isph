Welcome to ISPH 2026

The 2026 Annual Conference of the International Society for Precision Health (ISPH) will be held in Yilan, Taiwan, from October 21 to 23, 2026. This conference brings together leading scientists, industry experts, and policymakers from around the world to share knowledge and discuss the latest advances in precision health, nutrition, and disease prevention.

Since its establishment, ISPH has been dedicated to serving as an international platform for advancing precision health research. Its focus areas include nutraceuticals, dietary supplements, functional foods, and personalized nutrition interventions, with the goal of improving health outcomes and reducing disease risk. Through international conferences, online meetings, newsletters, and social media platforms, ISPH has successfully connected the scientific community, healthcare professionals, industry stakeholders, and the public, fostering interdisciplinary exchange and collaboration.

Last year's ISPH conference attracted approximately 524 participants from 52 countries worldwide. In addition, ISPH regularly organizes online thematic seminars to facilitate continuous knowledge sharing and professional engagement.

The organizing committee warmly invites participants from academia, industry, and regulatory agencies to join this exceptional opportunity for networking, collaboration, and high-quality scientific exchange in the beautiful setting of Yilan, Taiwan.
