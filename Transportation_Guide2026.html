Transportation Guide

To Kilin Hotel Jiaoxi (礁溪麒麟大飯店)

Hotel Address: No. 116, Sec. 5, Jiaoxi Rd., Jiaoxi Township, Yilan County 262, Taiwan
Phone: +886-3-988-8570
Website: kilinhotel.com

From Taoyuan International Airport (TPE)

Option 1: Direct Bus (Recommended)
  • Take Ubus #1661 (統聯客運) from TPE Terminal 1 or 2.
  • Destination: Jiaoxi Transfer Station (礁溪轉運站).
  • Travel Time: Approx. 90–120 minutes.
  • Cost: Approx. NT$200–250.
  • From the bus station, take a short taxi ride to the hotel (10 mins, approx. NT$120).
Option 2: Airport MRT + Train
  • Take Taoyuan Airport MRT to Taipei Main Station (35–40 min, NT$160).
  • Transfer to Taiwan Railway (TRA). Take an eastbound train to Jiaoxi Station.
  • Train Time: 60–90 minutes | Cost: NT$200–270.
  • From Jiaoxi Train Station, walk 5–8 minutes to the hotel.
Option 3: Private Car / Taxi
  • Direct from the airport to the hotel.
  • Time: 60–90 minutes.
  • Cost: NT$2,000–2,500 (Ensure price is agreed upon before departure).

From Taipei Songshan Airport (TSA)

Option 1: MRT + Train
  • Take the MRT (Subway) to Taipei Main Station (Requires transfer).
  • Transfer to Taiwan Railway (TRA) and take a train to Jiaoxi Station.
  • Total Time: 1.5–2 hours.
Option 2: Taxi
  • Direct from the airport to the hotel.
  • Time: 50–70 minutes | Cost: NT$1,500–1,800.

From Taipei City

Option 1: Bus (Fastest & Frequent)
  • From Taipei City Hall Station: Take Capital Bus #1572 (首都客運). Approx. 45-50 mins.
  • From Taipei Main Station (Q Square): Take Kamalan Bus #1915 (葛瑪蘭客運). Approx. 60 mins.
  • Cost: Approx. NT$130.
  • Buses arrive at Jiaoxi Transfer Station. Take a taxi (10 min) or walk (15-20 min) to the hotel.
Option 2: Taiwan Railway (Scenic)
  • Take a train from Taipei Main Station to Jiaoxi Station.
  • Express Train (Puyuma/Taroko/Tze-Chiang): 60–75 minutes | NT$263.
  • Local Train: 90–100 minutes | NT$128.
  • The hotel is just a 5-minute walk from the train station.

Last Mile: From Jiaoxi Train Station to Kilin Hotel

Walking Directions (Very Close - 5 mins)
  • Exit Jiaoxi Train Station and walk straight out.
  • Turn LEFT onto the main road (Jiaoxi Road Sec. 5).
  • Walk straight for about 450 meters.
  • The hotel will be on your LEFT side.
