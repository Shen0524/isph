Introduction to the ‚Äú9Health‚Äù Smart Nutrition Platform

Through an interactive questionnaire, the system collects individual health data and provides AI-powered real-time responses, image analysis, and member services. Users can upload meal photos to instantly estimate calories and six key nutrients‚Äîprotein, carbohydrates, fat, sodium, and fiber‚Äîand receive tailored dietary suggestions.
