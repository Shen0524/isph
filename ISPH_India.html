ISPH-India Has Launched

International Society for Precision Health - India Chapter

Advancing Precision Medicine Across Nations

Historic Launch • January 2026

A Momentous Occasion

The International Society for Precision Health proudly announces the establishment of its India chapter, marking a significant milestone in global precision health collaboration.

Presidential Celebration

Dr. Chun-Kun Wang, President of ISPH, attended the launching ceremony in person at Navrachana University, expressing his profound enthusiasm for this international expansion.

"This is tremendously exciting news following the establishment of ISPH in Taiwan just three months ago in October 2025. We are witnessing the rapid growth of a truly global movement in precision health."
