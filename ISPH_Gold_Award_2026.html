About the Award

The ISPH World Gold Award is established to promote product excellence in scientific rigor, quality, innovation, practical value, and international visibility. The award is granted on a competitive and merit-based basis, and may be withheld if no submission meets the required standards.

Eligibility

Any domestic or international company duly established in accordance with applicable laws may apply, provided that the submitted product aligns with the Society’s mission and reflects the principles of precision health, innovation, applicability, or social value.

  • Each company may submit one product only.
  • A completed application form and supporting documents are required.
  • Incomplete submissions may be remedied within the designated period; failure to do so will result in disqualification.
Unsuccessful applicants will receive a 50% refund of the application fee, except in cases such as withdrawal after registration, false documentation, or failure to cooperate with the review process.

Review Criteria

  1. Alignment with the principles and vision of precision health
  2. Scientific basis, technical foundation, and evidence-based support
  3. Product quality, safety, and credibility
  4. Innovation, market applicability, and development potential
  5. Contribution to health promotion, social well-being, or industry advancement

Suggested Materials

  • Product description and differentiating features
  • Scientific evidence and research-based support
  • Inspection, testing, or quality management records
  • R&D outcomes or other relevant review materials

Announcement

Announcement: Applications Open for the 1st ISPH World Gold Award 2026 The International Society for Precision Health (ISPH) is pleased to announce that applications are now officially open for the 1st ISPH World Gold Award. This award is established to recognize and promote outstanding products that embody the principles of precision health and demonstrate excellence in scientific evidence, product quality, innovation, practical application, and social value. Eligible domestic and international companies are invited to submit one product per application cycle. All submissions will be reviewed by a professional Review Committee formed by ISPH, composed of qualified scholars and experts from relevant fields. Application deadline: 30 June 2026. Evaluation will be based on the following dimensions: alignment with precision health principles, scientific and technical foundation, quality and safety, innovation and market applicability, and contribution to health promotion or industry advancement. The application fee is USD 10,000 per product, or USD 5,000 per product for ISPH organizational members. Applicants whose products are not selected will receive a 50% refund, except in cases such as withdrawal, false documentation, or failure to cooperate with the review process. Award-winning products will retain their award status for five years from the date of ISPH’s official announcement and may use the award title and insignia in accordance with the Society’s regulations. ISPH warmly welcomes outstanding companies from around the world to participate and join us in advancing precision health through excellence and innovation.
